HIVE Work


For my year in industry I worked in Hulls Immersive Visualisation Environemt (HIVE), which is situated within the University of Hulls Computer Science department. During my time there I worked on developing virtual reality softwares, as well as demonstrating to school visits and companies. As well as this we demonstrated our software at a number of events, such as, University of Hulls open day/applicant days, Hulls Science Festival and London Met Science Expo.


Turbine Descent

This is a virtual reality emergency descent down a wind turbine, designed to supplement the Working at Heights training provided by Humberside Fire and Rescue Solutions.


Sprue Alarm Alert

This is an alert system developed for Hessle Fire and Rescue. When a Sprue alarm goes off, it sends an alert to this interface. It then sends an acknoweldgement when it has been received (accepted by someone in the command room) and stores it in a database.


Virtual Incident Command

This is a virtual reality training package for training and testing Incident Commanders, developed for Humberside Fire and Rescue.
